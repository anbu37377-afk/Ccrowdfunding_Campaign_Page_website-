Who We Are

Who We Are

Empowering Ideas to Change the World

Founded in 2024, FundRise started with a simple question: "What if anyone with a great idea could find the support they need?"

We believe in the power of the crowd. Traditional funding is gatekept, hard to access, and often disconnected from the community. We are changing that by building a platform rooted in **trust, transparency, and connection**.
